A mid-sized steel fabrication company was facing 35% annual turnover and rising rework costs. By implementing RWRM’s pillars of Algorithmic Alignment and Generational Flow Integration, MDOA Solutions helped:

  • Save $1.2M annually in labor and training costs
  • Improve productivity by 22% without additional headcount

Result: Apex Structures is now an employer of choice in its region with long-term workforce stability.
